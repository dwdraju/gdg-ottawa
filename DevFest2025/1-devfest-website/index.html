About DevFest Ottawa 2025

Code. Connect. Create. — Ottawa’s biggest developer conference of the year.

DevFest Ottawa is a one-day community-run conference bringing together Googlers, Google Developer Experts (GDEs), and local tech leaders for hands-on talks and practical sessions. Join us on Sunday, November 2nd, 2025 from 9:30 AM–4:30 PM to learn, collaborate, and build.

  • What to expect: Keynotes, technical sessions, workshops, and networking with peers.
  • Topics: Cloud, AI/ML, Web, Mobile, Flutter, Gemini, Model Context Protocol and more.
  • Included: Light breakfast, coffee, lunch, and swag.
  • Who should attend: Students, professional developers, and anyone curious about modern developer tooling and AI.

Location: Room #C240, Learning Crossroads (CRX), 100 Louis-Pasteur Private, Ottawa, ON K1N 9N3. See parking: uOttawa parking.
